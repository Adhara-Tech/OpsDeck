How Automation Rules Work

Automation rules allow the system to automatically evaluate compliance based on recorded evidence (activities, campaigns, tests).

  • Green (Compliant): Evidence found within the frequency period
  • Yellow (Warning): Evidence is past frequency but within grace period
  • Red (Non-Compliant): Evidence is past both frequency and grace period
